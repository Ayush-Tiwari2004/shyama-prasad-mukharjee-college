Chairperson's Message
Prof. Pankaj Kumar

I am happy to learn that SPM Govt Degree college, Phaphamau, Allahabad is redesigning its Website to make it more user friendly , interactive and worthy of the expectations of the stakeholders of higher education. Websites can serve its purpose only when it is dynamic, update, categorically state institution's academic profile , vision, mission, achievements and perspective plans to fulfil aspirations of society, students as well. In view of the recruitments made in the preceding academic session, the college has more serious responsibility to develop website in such a way that academic strength, infrastructure support, basic amenities and welfare schemes of the college are fully refleced in the website to enable a aspiring students to select courses of his choice . Hope the website of the college to be designed to be accessible and accountable to its user stakeholders.
